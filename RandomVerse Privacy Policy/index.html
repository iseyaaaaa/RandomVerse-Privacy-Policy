RandomVerse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Welcome to RandomVerse! Your privacy is incredibly important to us. In fact, we like your privacy so much that we decided to completely leave it alone. Because RandomVerse is a simple, straightforward tool designed to give you a random Bible verse at the tap of a button, we have built the app to operate without collecting any of your personal information.

    1. Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location).
  • Account Creation: There is no sign-in or account creation required to use RandomVerse. You open the app, and it just works.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your app usage, what verses you look at, or how long you spend in the app.

    2. Cookies and Third-Party Services

  • Cookies: RandomVerse does not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • Third-Party SDKs: We do not use third-party analytics tools, advertisement networks, or data brokers that track you across the internet.

3. Children’s Privacy

Because RandomVerse does not collect any personal data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect personal information from children.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy if we add new features in the future. However, our core philosophy of respecting your data will remain the same. If we update it, the new "Effective Date" will be posted at the top of this page.Ç
